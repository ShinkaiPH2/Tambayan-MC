Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 31, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Tambayan MC. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Minecraft server, Discord bot, and website (collectively, "Services"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

Information We Collect

1. Minecraft Server

When you join our Minecraft server, we may collect:

  • Minecraft Username: Your in-game username
  • UUID: Your unique Minecraft player identifier
  • IP Address: For security and moderation purposes
  • Chat Messages: In-game chat logs for moderation
  • Gameplay Data: Player statistics, coordinates, inventory, playtime, achievements
  • Connection Data: Login times, session duration, server version

2. Discord Bot

When you interact with our Discord bot, we may collect:

  • Discord User ID: Your unique Discord identifier
  • Discord Username: Your Discord username and discriminator
  • Server Information: Server ID, channel ID where commands are used
  • Command Data: Commands you execute and associated parameters
  • Message Content: Only when necessary for bot functionality
  • Role Information: Your roles in Discord servers where the bot operates

3. Website

When you visit our website, we may collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent, click behavior
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • IP Address: For analytics and security purposes
  • Cookies: To enhance user experience (see Cookie Policy below)

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Security & Moderation: To enforce rules, prevent cheating, and maintain a safe environment
  • Communication: To send important updates, announcements, and respond to inquiries
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with our Services
  • Account Linking: To link Minecraft and Discord accounts for integrated features
  • Community Management: To manage player rankings, leaderboards, and statistics
  • Technical Support: To troubleshoot issues and provide customer support

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share data in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party hosting and infrastructure providers
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Public Display: Certain data (usernames, statistics) may be publicly visible on leaderboards
  • With Your Consent: We may share data with your explicit permission

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our Services:

  • Active Players: Data retained while you actively use our Services
  • Inactive Players: Data may be retained for up to 1 year after last activity
  • Banned Users: Essential data retained to enforce bans
  • Chat Logs: Moderation logs retained for up to 90 days

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

Children's Privacy

Our Services comply with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you are under 13, please have a parent or guardian contact us before using our Services.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. We use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand usage patterns
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Anti-cheat and anti-bot protection

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

Our Services may integrate with third-party platforms:

  • Discord: Subject to Discord's Privacy Policy
  • Mojang/Microsoft: Minecraft account data subject to their policies
  • Hosting Providers: Server hosting and infrastructure services

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our Services constitutes acceptance of changes.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact us:

  • Discord: Join our Discord server and open a support ticket
  • In-Game: Contact server administrators
  • Website: Use the contact form on our website
